1. Information We Collect

GrapeDown Download Manager does notstrong> collect any personal information. Specifically, the application does NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Browsing history or download history
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Any data transmitted to external servers operated by the developer

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted elsewhere:

  • Error Logs:strong> Crash and error logs saved to %LOCALAPPDATA%\GrapeDown\grapedown_error.log for debugging purposes. This file is stored only on your device and is never sent anywhere.
  • Downloaded Files: Files downloaded by the application are stored in the folder you choose. The application does not access these files for any other purpose.

3. Network Connections

GrapeDown makes outbound network connections solely for the purpose you request:

  • File Downloads: Connects to URLs you provide to download files.
  • Torrent Downloads: Connects to BitTorrent peer networks for the torrent links you provide.
  • Local Browser Extension Communication: Runs a local HTTP server on 127.0.0.1:65432 (and fallback ports) to receive download requests from our browser extension. This communication never leaves your device.

The application does not connect to any telemetry, analytics, or advertising services.

4. Browser Extension

The GrapeDown browser extension communicates only with the local GrapeDown application running on your device (via 127.0.0.1). No data is sent to external servers. The extension intercepts download links you click and forwards them to the local GrapeDown application.

5. Third-Party Services

GrapeDown does not integrate with, nor share data with, any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

6. Children's Privacy

GrapeDown is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

Since GrapeDown does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data at risk of breach. All network communication for downloads uses the protocols (HTTP/HTTPS/FTP) provided by the URL you enter.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
